About us

Wild Trails and Travel is a company registered in Kenya and based in Nairobi. This is the gateway to a one stop wildlife experience, fantastic holiday destinations, adventure and unforgettable life experiences. Wild Trails & Travel specializes in holiday planning and brings the holiday-maker in touch with “wilderness hospitality” in the authentic African setting. At Wild Trails & Travel we package your ‘dream holiday’ in style, taking into consideration the travelers’ interests, budget and time. We cater for all travelers’ needs through mix and balance considering; the Pace, Route, Interest, Details of the trail and the Energy levels of the traveler. Our ultimate goal is to give the holiday-maker leisure, pleasure and unforgettable life experiences. Our Trails come in style, more wild oriented from National Parks, cultural trails, exotic beach holidays to wild accommodation in the tented camps and lodges that give a natural feel of nature. Our professionals plan distinctive and perfect holiday destinations, catering for all age groups on escorted tours, independent, or hosted tours, with a choice of pre-packaged or customer-made trails. Our Mission is to promote International and Local tourism, while encouraging and promoting “Responsible Travel and Tourism” with emphasis on the importance for respect of all destinations. As we create memorable experiences, we also touch the hearts of the less fortunate in society by making donations to children’s Homes.
